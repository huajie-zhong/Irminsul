# The Three Laws of Maintenance

These are the operational consequences of the core principles. They are simple enough to check mechanically. When something feels wrong with a doc, ask which law it's violating. The answer almost always points at the fix.

> **Law 1.** Each fact has exactly one home.
>
> **Law 2.** Each document has exactly one purpose and one audience moment.
>
> **Law 3.** Every cross-reference is bidirectional and machine-verifiable.
